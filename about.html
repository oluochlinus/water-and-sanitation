Who We Are

We impact the lives of people from across the country by making affordable the basic commodity (water) through provision of a digital platform that is a one-stop-shop for all water related information as well as products right from the immediate water service provider within any locality to an online shop where anyone can purchase various products at affordable rates while considering the quality standards, notwithstanding the various loan products for such commodities as well. while it is currently the water tanks that can be purchased through a credit facility, we intend to inlude other products like the submersible water pumps and solar panels to help accelerate uptake of clean and safe water while providing minimum time in drawing water at the household level

Many households in both urban and rural areas do not have access to clean water due to high cost of related products - a problem we intend to solve with our patners

Africa and Kenya in particular is faced by challenges of providing clean water to all homes in both urban and rural areas as a matter of priority to help mitigate the water-borne diseases as well as solve the poverty puzzle by providing water to rural homes for agricultural purposes throughout the year.
